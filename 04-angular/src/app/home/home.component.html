Sharing the Natural Bounty of the Bay

Welcome! We are a family-owned farm and provisioner of fine nuts, dried fruits, popped corn, and other farm-fresh delicacies since 2007.

Blessed with a year-round sunny, idyllic Mediterranean climate, the San Francisco Bay is a natural paradise known not only for its world-class vineries, but also for its rich abundance of fruits, nuts, and other agricultural treasures which are exported and enjoyed worldwide.

We are proud to present premium quality, organic specialty goods straight from our orchards in the heart of the Bay to your family table.
